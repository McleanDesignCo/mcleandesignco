"Josh helped to give our business a much needed professional look! He designed all of our branding and logos which we love! ."
Sewing Quarter was a TV crafting channel dedicated to sewing, quilting and cross stitch. It was broadcast live every day from 8am-1pm on Freeview channel 73, Sky channel 687 or on their website.
My role as Graphic Designer was spent developing the Sewing Quarter brand across product design, packaging, event livery, Live TV & Broadcast Graphics, social media graphics, print design for various magazine outlets such as radio times.
From Creating Huge Event Banners, TV Ads, Merchandise including enamel pin badges and 'Best Selling' Christmas Advent calendars packed with crafting goodies.

  • Challenge

    Create html email campaigns that are in line with all B&O branding.

  • Solution

    Use B&O brand guidelines to marry the html emails to the web brand.

  • Result

    Improved brand implementation across email channels for clearer customer engagement & high converting sales.
